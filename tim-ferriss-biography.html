    Who is Tim Ferriss?

    Tim Ferriss is an American author, entrepreneur and public speaker. Tim has many books on the 4 Hour theme. His many books had appeared on the bestseller list of USA Today, Wall Street Journal and New York Times. Apart from it Tim is also an advisor and angel investor to Facebook, Twitter, Evernote, StumbleUpon, and Uber.

    What is the birth name of Tim Ferriss?

    Tim Ferriss was born on July 20, 1977, in East Hampton, New York, USA. His nationality is American and ethnicity is unknown. His birth name is Timothy “Tim” Ferriss. Tim’s father is a real estate agent and mother a  physical therapist.

    Tim Ferriss: Education, School/College University

    He studied at St. Paul’s School in Concord, New Hampton and cleared his graduation there. Tim also traveled to Japan as an exchange student while he was in his junior years.

    Later Tim Ferriss went to Princeton University due to his strength in application essay and achieved his degree in East Asian Studies

    Tim Ferriss: Professional Life and Career

    Tim Ferriss Started his career in Sales at a data storage company along with his own internet business, BrainQUICKEN. Ferriss made his first foray into publishing with the audiobook Tim Ferriss Publishing. Tim Started his own nutritional supplement company which turned into a successful venture, BodyQUICK.

    Tim also worked for television as a pilot in “Trial By Fire” on History Channel, where he learned the skills which are normally studied over the course of many years. He also practiced yabusame (horseback archery) in the pilot episode. Tim also shot 13 episodes of The Tim Ferriss Experiment and debuted in HLN (Headlines News). Although a short portion of the show was shown on Television. Tim also published books including Vagabonding by Rolf Potts, The Obstacle is the Way by Ryan Holiday, What I Learned Losing a Million Dollars by Jim Paul and Brendan Moynihan.

    Tim Ferriss is also an investor and angel advisor including StumbleUpon, Posterous, Evernote, DailyBurn, Shopify, Reputation.com, Trippy and TaskRabbit. He is a pre-seed money advisor to Uber. Tim was also listed among “Notable Angel Investors” in The New York Times.

    Tim Ferriss is also an author of the books including The 4-Hour Workweek and The 4-Hour Body which was No. 1 New York Time bestseller, The 4-Hour Chef which was No. 1 Wall Street Journal bestseller. Tim also wrote Tools of Titans. and holds a Guinness World Record in Argentine Tango, a musical genre of simple quadruple meter and binary musical form, and the social dance that accompanies it.
