    If you’ve been keeping up with all the news regarding Marvel Studios as of late, then you no doubt know that the brand is expanding beyond cinemas and onto the Disney Plus streaming service. And leading that charge will be The Falcon and the Winter Soldier, which understandably has True Believers around the world intrigued.

    Recommended Videos

    Of course, much of the show’s appeal comes from seeing Sam Wilson now wielding the shield after accepting the Captain America mantle back in Avengers: Endgame. After all, the concept of legacy heroes hasn’t been explored too often in the live-action realm, so it should be interesting to see how things play out here.

    Unfortunately, we still don’t have a trailer for The Falcon and the Winter Soldier or even any official stills, but today we’ve managed to get our hands on the next best thing, a poster straight from D23. And you can check it out for yourself down below.

    It’s not the best quality, sure, but it’s clear enough to see how Sam and Bucky will appear in the series and the latter, in particular, is sporting a pretty interesting new look. Well, we shouldn’t say “new,” as this is how the character appeared in WWII, but we haven’t seen him with his hair slicked back in a while and we’re curious to learn why he’s returned to this style.

    With The Falcon and the Winter Soldier set to hit Disney Plus in the Fall of 2020, it shouldn’t be too much longer now before things really ramp up and with any luck, we may just get our first proper look at it before the year is out. Until then, though, enjoy the poster up above and stay tuned for more.
