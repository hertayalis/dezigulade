    25 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Once Princess Anne and her bull terrier step out together, it's over for y'all.

    The second child and only daughter of the Queen and Prince Philip was spotted with her dog at the Festival of the British Evening at Gatcombe Park, her estate in the English countryside today, looking precious as ever. The Princess Royal sported casual neutrals with a bucket hat and sunglasses, while her four-legged friend stuck his head out the window to say hi (and possibly flaunt his chain collar).

    DAVID HARTLEY/REX/Shutterstock

    Princess Anne's pet isn't the only one on festival grounds. In addition to the horse races and showcases at the event, there are also dog agility runs, dog shows, and presentations by The Royal Agricultural University Beagles and The Duke of Beaufort's Hounds. So basically, there are cute pups all around.

    This is the greatest royal-and-dog-in-car combo we've seen since the Queen brought Meghan Markle's dog to Windsor Castle. Days ahead of the royal wedding, the Duchess of Sussex's dog, Guy, was spotted sitting next to Her Majesty en route to the venue.

    Princess Anne's pups have previously made cameos at Gatcombe Park for the Whatley Manor Horse Trials and out on a walk through the grass with their royal owner.

The adorable young trio indulged in ice cream for the fun family outing.
