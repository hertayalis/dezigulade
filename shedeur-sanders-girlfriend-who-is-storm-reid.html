    Shedeur Sanders is a American football quarterback, he is currently a freshman at Jackson State University. He is the son of Hall of Fame NFL player Deion Sanders. Shedeur started his high school football career at Trinity Christian School in Cedar Hill, Texas, where he set multiple school records and led the team to the state championship game in 2018.

    He was then transferred to Bradenton’s IMG Academy in Florida for his senior year, where he helped lead the team to a 9-1 record. He was rated as a four-star prospect and one of the top dual-threat quarterbacks in the country. He is committed to play college football at Jackson State University, where his father is now head coach. He was redshirted in the 2020 season.
