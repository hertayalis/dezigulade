Biography Dr. Kenneth Hepps was born in Los Angeles and is a 3rd generation Californian. After completing his undergraduate studies at Duke University, Dr. Hepps returned to LA to attend medical school and residency at UCLA. He subsequently completed a prestigious fellowship program in Gastroenterology at Baylor Medical College. Additionally, he completed fellowship training in Biliary Endoscopy and ERCP. Dr. Hepps’ medical expertise includes complex therapeutic endoscopic cases, treatment of hepatitis C, inflammatory bowel disease, colon cancer screening and treatment, gastro reflux and ulcer management, liver and pancreatic problems, and other digestive health issues.
