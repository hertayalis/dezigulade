    Meg Ryan is an American actress and producer. She was born on November 19, 1961, in Fairfield, Connecticut.

    Ryan began her acting career in the 1980s with roles in popular movies such as “Top Gun” and “When Harry Met Sally.” She quickly became known for her girl-next-door charm and romantic-comedy roles.

    Ryan’s most notable films include “Sleepless in Seattle,” “You’ve Got Mail,” “City of Angels,” and “The Women.” She has been nominated for several awards throughout her career, including Golden Globe nominations for her performances in “When Harry Met Sally,” “Sleepless in Seattle,” and “You’ve Got Mail.”

    In addition to acting, Ryan has also worked as a producer and director. She made her directorial debut in 2015 with the film “Ithaca,” in which she also starred.

    Ryan has been recognized for her philanthropic work and has supported various causes, including breast cancer research, education, and children’s rights. She has two children and was previously married to actor Dennis Quaid.

    Does Meg Ryan have any children?

    Meg Ryan has two children, a son named Jack Quaid and a daughter named Daisy True Ryan.

    Jack Quaid was born on April 24, 1992, in Los Angeles, California, and is Meg Ryan’s son with her ex-husband, actor Dennis Quaid. Jack Quaid is also an actor, known for his roles in movies such as “The Hunger Games” and “Logan Lucky”, as well as the TV series “The Boys”.
