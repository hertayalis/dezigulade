    Having the right kids shoes for the beach and pool makes all the difference

      Summer holiday season is here, and if the recent heatwave has got you thinking about holiday shopping, you've come to the right place.

      Whether your holiday plans centre around paddling in the pool, swimming in the sea or frolicking in streams and lakes, a good pair of water shoes for children makes all the difference.

      Scroll on for our edit of the best beach and water shoes for kids and prepare for your best summer ever.

      How we chose the best kids' beach shoes for summer 2023

      • Price: We looked for shoes that were the most affordable - kids grow so quickly that realistically these shoes will be good for a single summer only. You don’t want to be spending an arm and a leg on them
      • Ease of putting on and taking off: We wanted above all to find shoes that little people would have no problem putting on and taking off themselves.
      • Design: Fun, bright shoes were the order of the day - summer is all about having fun, after all!
      • Non-slip: Safety was a big concern. Since the children will be wearing these to run around in wet areas they had to have good grips to avoid accidents
      • Durable: All the shoes we considered were sturdy and durable enough to withstand endless days of running around, jumping about and playing.
      • Easy to clean: Since these shoes are going to get wet, muddy and sandy they had to be all of the wipe / wash clean under the tap variety

      Best kids beach shoes for summer 2023

      M&S Kids Monster Crocs

      £10 AT MARKS & SPENCER

      Available sizes: Children's size 4 to children’s size 13

      These clogs are a favourite with M&S shoppers, who often buy multiple pairs. They’re waterproof, grip proof and easy to clean. You can shop the full range of Marks & Spencer beach clogs here. 

      Editors note: "These shoes are super fun and are less than half price than RRP of Crocs, which are basically the same, but last a little longer. I've bought M&S clogs several years running for my two and they do look quite worn out by the end of the summer, but it's never been an issue since they had outgrown them by next year anyway."

      Decathalon Elasticated Aqua Shoes 120 for Kids

      £12.99 AT DECATHALON

      Available sizes: Children’s size 9.5 to adult's size 2.5

      These elasticated Aquashoes are designed to be worn while swimming in the sea and exploring rocks on the shore – they remove the danger of little ones cutting their feet on sharp stones. They come in a wide range of colours to keep everyone happy.

      Editor's note: "Decathalon in general is our go-to for sporting essentials for kids and the adults in the family alike. We've had these shoes in the past for the kids to go wading in the sea with when there were pebbles and rocks on the sea bed that could have hurt their feet. They're easy to get on and off and dry really quickly."

      Crocs Unisex Classic Kids' Clogs

      FROM £12 AT AMAZON

      Available sizes: Children’s size 2 to adult size 10

      Crocs are the original beach shoe that spawned all the copies. The advantage of shelling out for the real deal is they tend to last longer and wear much better than other models. They’re a bit more pricey, but you can find bargains on Amazon, like the ones above. They’re available in a rainbow of colours, but some colours are more expensive than others, so check before you add to basket!

      Editor's note: “Crocs are the shoes we just keep coming back to year after year. Everyone in the family owns a pair, even the adults! I tend to size up with Crocs in an attempt to get them to last a couple of years - they're so durable that they will last. When compared to 'own brand' imitations at the end of the summer, you can see the difference - the Crocs hold up much better."

      There's also currently a sale on at the Crocs online store, so you can snap up some bargains at up to 50% off. The kids' baya crocs are 30% off and available in blue, navy or pink.
