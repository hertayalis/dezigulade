    • In a clip from an upcoming episode of Keeping Up with the Kardashians, Kris Jenner tends to her youngest daughter, Kylie Jenner, as the beauty mogul recovers from Lasik eye surgery.
    • Kylie hilariously uses a tiny bell to summon her mother for assistance.

    Kylie Jenner relied on the help of her mother, Kris Jenner, as she recovered from Lasik eye surgery—as well as the ringing of a dainty bell to signal her every want and need.

    In a clip for this Thursday's episode of Keeping Up with the Kardashians, Kris, in true momager fashion, waits hand and foot on her youngest daughter.

    "She's a bit out of it, so I just want to make sure she's okay, because the healthiest thing she can do right now is to get some rest," Kris says in the short clip before setting the bell by Kylie's bedside as she rests.

    Kylie quickly takes to her new signaling device, however, and poses a few very specific requests—not limited to water with no ice and lemon, and tacos for a post-surgery lunch. Clearly a bit lethargic post-procedure, Kylie continues to ring for assistance, as Kris whips up a batch of tacos for her billionaire beauty mogul daughter. Jenner gets her cheeky revenge, of course, teasing her daughter by taking away a taco each time Kylie attempts to take a bed-bound bite of her lunch.

    More of Kylie's post-procedure experience was documented via her best friend Stassie Karanikolaou's YouTube channel, where she also recorded the two opting in for the eye surgery.

    "My sight has been born," Jenner jokes in the video.
