    11 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Selena Gomez and her boyfriend Benny Blanco had their own personal backyard photoshoot on the Fourth of July.

    Gomez—who usually spends the holiday weekend with her bestie, Taylor Swift—looked radiant in photos she (and Blanco) shared on Instagram. In the snaps, the Only Murders in the Building star can be seen wearing a flirty white sundress with vertical pleats and cartoon-like doodles, including an orange heart, pink flower, and yellow stars.

    Instagram / @itsbennyblanco//Instagram

    She paired the summery dress with gold hoops and a thick white headband, which kept her wavy black hair out of her face. The Y2K hair accessory added a youthful glow to her look, as did her sun-kissed skin. For the laid-back occasion, she opted for a natural glam that included sculpted brows, wispy lashes, and a pink lipstick.

    Instagram / @itsbennyblanco//Instagram

    While Gomez’s breezy seasonal style was on display, so was her feelings for Blanco. In another photo shared to her Instagram story, she grinned as her boyfriend wrapped his arms around her, making it clear the pair couldn’t be happier.

    Courtesy of Instagram/@selenagomez//Instagram

    Gomez first met the music producer way back in 2015, when they worked together on her second solo studio album, Revival—but it wasn’t until 2023 that their relationship went from professional to PDA. Gomez soft-launched their relationship on her Instagram, where she shared a photo of herself laying on Blanco’s chest. Since then, the couple has kept their dating life fairly public, even hinting at marriage and children.

    Related Stories

    Blanco, who recently appeared on The Howard Stern Show, took the opportunity to open up about his future with Gomez. When Stern commented “I'm predicting marriage,” Blanco replied without hesitation, “That's the plan.” The music producer later said that children are his “next goal on the box.” He added, “I've got a lotta good kids, I've got a ton of nephews. It's my thing. I love being around kids.”
