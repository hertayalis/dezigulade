    19 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Rihanna made her mark Friday night, set in a cool, sporty look and ready to go. She stunted an athletic street style outfit while catching a flight to Las Vegas for 2023 Formula One Weekend.

    The “Work It” singer was photographed in Michael Jordan’s red-and-black Chicago Bulls jersey, a black satin bomber jacket, and a black maxi cargo skirt with a center slit. She paired the look with white ankle socks and her Fenty Puma Creepers—a black suede platform sneaker with white accents and a gum sole—and accessorized with a black Gucci Horsebit bag and big black sunglasses. Repping her beau A$AP Rocky, she wore a red-and-white trucker hat with blue lettering across the front that read “AWGE,” the rapper’s creative agency, over her straight honey-blonde hair. She added some bling with a gold choker and diamond necklace and brought a neon pop of color to the look with green nails.

    The “Love on the Brain” singer traded sporty for glam on Monday when she coordinated in all-black looks with A$AP Rocky. She stepped out in Los Angeles in a velvet jumpsuit from Alaïa, a long leather coat, and eye-catching black satin heels with sparkling crystal wraparound straps from Amina Muaddi. The rapper kept the velvet and leather going in a leather jacket, baggy black trousers, and a velvet shirt tied around his waist.

    The Fenty Beauty founder will watch 20 of the world’s fastest drivers fire up their engines on Saturday to race on a new Strip Circuit that uses some of the busiest streets in Las Vegas.
